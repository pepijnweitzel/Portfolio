sort1 uses: bubble sort

How do you know?: Fastest to sort with an already sorted file, but slower when sorting non sorted files.

sort2 uses: merge sort

How do you know?: Lowest average sorting time, order of the numbers do not matter much to the sorting technique but will still sort it faster than bubble sort and selection sort.

sort3 uses: selection sort

How do you know?: The order of the numbers in the files do not matter to the sorting time, just as in selection sort. It will always take the same number of steps to sort.
