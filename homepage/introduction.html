Meet me, a Dutch explorer

Hello there! My name is Pepijn Weitzel, and I'm thrilled to share a bit about myself with you. I believe life is a grand adventure, and I'm always on the lookout for new experiences and knowledge.
Here's a glimpse into my story:

I hail from the beautiful Netherlands, where I embarked on the challenging TTO VWO program during high school. This rigorous educational journey fueled my passion
for learning and broadened my horizons. It's amazing how much you can discover when you're encouraged to explore. After completing high school, I decided to take a giant leap into the unknown.
My girlfriend and I embarked on an incredible 11-month world trip. We roamed through bustling cities, trekked in serene mountains, and soaked up the cultures of diverse countries.
This journey shaped me in ways I could have never imagined, fostering my curiosity and a deep appreciation for the beauty of our planet. As an eternal seeker of knowledge,
I'm currently diving into the world of computer science through Harvard's CS50 Introduction to Computer Science course. The digital realm offers endless possibilities, and I can't wait to explore
the depths of coding and technology. I came into this world on the 8th of June in 2004, and since then, every birthday has been a reminder of the wonderful adventure called life.
I'm always eager to celebrate another year of growth, learning, and discovery. Curiosity is my driving force. I believe that the pursuit of knowledge, understanding, and experience is what makes life truly fulfilling.
Whether it's delving into the mysteries of the universe, learning a new language, or exploring a hidden gem in my own backyard, I'm always ready to embrace the next adventure.

So, that's a little about me, a Dutch explorer with a passion for learning, a love for travel, and an insatiable curiosity about the world and the endless possibilities it holds.
I'm excited to connect, learn, and share experiences with you as we navigate this exciting journey called life together.
