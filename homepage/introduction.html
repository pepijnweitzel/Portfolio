Introducing, me

A curious Dutch kid. Raised in a small town called Reek, I was always seeking for adventure. Either something in the outside world or in the online world.
Not knowing what to do, I went on a world-travel-adventure just when I finished high school, visiting places like New-Zealand, Japan, UAE, and many more, all this while having one goal in mind, finding purpose.
Together with my girlfriend Celeste I was figuring out extraordinary ways to succeed in life, not accepting and following the ordinairy route of going to university. Which in the end brought me to CS50.
I found out I was quiet good in learning things on my own when i was just a kid, not paying too much attention at school but still getting excellent marks.
